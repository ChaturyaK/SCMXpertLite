Supply Chain Management (SCM) is the end-to-end process of designing, planning, executing, and controlling the efficient flow of goods, services, and information from the point of origin to the end consumer.It involves coordinating various elements, including procurement, production, transportation, and distribution, to optimize efficiency, reduce costs, and enhance customer satisfaction. SCM aims to create a seamless and responsive network that adapts to market demands, minimizes lead times, and ensures a synchronized flow of products through each stage of the supply chain, fostering collaboration among suppliers, manufacturers, distributors, and retailers.
